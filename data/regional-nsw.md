# Designated Regional Areas — New South Wales

> **Source:** Australian Government, Department of Home Affairs — [Designated regional area postcodes](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/working-in-australia/skill-occupation-list/regional-postcodes)
> **Retrieved:** 2026-07-07
> **Applies to:** Skilled Work Regional (subclass 491), Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (494), and the 491→191 permanent-residence pathway.
> ⚠️ **Guidance only — not immigration advice.** Verify against the Home Affairs source before relying on it for a visa decision. Policy/postcodes can change.

## The rule in plain English

For NSW, a **designated regional area = everywhere in the state EXCEPT Greater Sydney.**

Home Affairs splits regional Australia into two categories — **and BOTH count as "regional"** for the 491/494 visas:

- **Category 2 — Cities and Major Regional Centres.** For NSW: **Newcastle/Lake Macquarie** and **Wollongong/Illawarra** (plus the Central Coast fringe). These *are* regional for visa purposes (a common misconception is that they aren't).
- **Category 3 — Regional Centres and Other Regional Areas.** The rest of regional NSW.

The **only** NSW postcodes that are **NOT** regional are Greater Sydney (Category 1 — the major-city exclusion).

## NSW postcode ranges

### Category 2 — Cities & Major Regional Centres (regional ✓)
```
2259, 2264–2308, 2500–2526, 2528–2535, 2574
```
Covers: Newcastle, Lake Macquarie, Maitland, Cessnock, Wollongong, Shellharbour, Kiama, parts of the Central Coast.

### Category 3 — Regional Centres & Other Regional Areas (regional ✓)
```
2250–2258, 2260–2263, 2311–2490, 2527, 2536–2551,
2575–2739, 2753–2754, 2756–2758, 2773–2898
```
Covers: Central Coast (Gosford), Hunter Valley, Mid North Coast, Northern Rivers (Coffs Harbour, Lismore, Byron, Ballina, Tweed), New England (Tamworth, Armidale), Shoalhaven, South Coast (Nowra, Batemans Bay, Bega, Eden), Southern Highlands, Riverina/Murray (Wagga Wagga, Albury, Griffith), Central West (Orange, Bathurst, Dubbo), Far West (Broken Hill).

### NOT regional — Greater Sydney (Category 1, excluded ✗)
Everything **not** listed above, i.e. approximately:
```
2000–2249, 2260*, 2555–2573, 2740–2752, 2755, 2759–2772
```
Covers: Sydney CBD, Inner/Greater Sydney metro, Blue Mountains fringe, Penrith/Hawkesbury metro band, Camden/Campbelltown metro.

> The machine-readable ranges powering the app's auto-tagging live in [`regional-nsw-postcodes.json`](./regional-nsw-postcodes.json). The tagging function treats **Category 2 ∪ Category 3** as `isRegionalNSW = true`.

## What this means for Manish (485 → 491 pathway)

- **Wollongong (2500) — his base — is regional ✓.** He can build 491-qualifying regional work/residence history without relocating.
- The biggest regional-NSW tech/IT job pools are **Wollongong (2500)**, **Newcastle (2300)**, and the **Central Coast (2250)** — all Category 2, all regional.
- Any job the tracker tags **non-regional** sits in Greater Sydney and does **not** count toward the 491 pathway.
