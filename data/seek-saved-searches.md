## Why region-scoped, not city + radius
A city + 50 km radius bleeds into Greater Sydney (non-regional): e.g. Gosford + 50 km returned **1,936** jobs (mostly northern Sydney) vs **12** region-scoped. Wollongong + 50 km returned 35 (incl. Sutherland/Sydney) vs **19** region-scoped. Region entities keep alerts genuinely regional → 491-relevant.

## How to add more regional hubs (if widening the relocation net)
On any SEEK results page: set classification = ICT, click the **location** box, type the region name, pick the **"…NSW"** region option (NOT a specific suburb+postcode), Submit, then **"Save this search"**. Candidate inland regions:
- Wagga Wagga & Riverina NSW
- Albury & Murray NSW
- Bathurst & Central West NSW / Orange & Central West
- Dubbo & Western NSW
- Coffs Harbour & North Coast NSW
- Lismore & Far North Coast NSW
- Tamworth & North West NSW
- Southern Highlands & Tablelands NSW

## Notes / caveats
- **Skip security-clearance roles** (e.g. Leidos "TSPV/DISA — Australian citizens only") — not visa-eligible.
- The **operational cash-flow track** (retail/cleaning/kitchen) is NOT in these ICT searches by design — set up separately if wanted.
- SEEK has no candidate API, so these native saved-search email alerts are the sustainable discovery engine; the tracker app ingests matches from them (+ LinkedIn/Indeed) via manual add / paste-URL / the planned browser extension.
